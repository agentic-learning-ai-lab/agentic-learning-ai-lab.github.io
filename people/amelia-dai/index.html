Amelia (Hui) Dai

Master Student

Amelia (Hui) Dai is a PhD student at University of Chicago. She obtained her Master's degree at NYU Center for Data Science, working with Prof. Mengye Ren in the Agentic Learning AI Lab and Prof. Krzysztof J. Geras. Her research interests include understanding LLMs (capabilities and limitations) and AI in healthcare. Recently, her work includes evaluating the generalization ability of LLMs and multi-modal learning for breast cancer detection. Previously, she completed her BS in Statistics at The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen. Outside of school, she also does hiphop dancing!
